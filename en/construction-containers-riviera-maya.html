Why shipping containers?

Shipping containers were originally created and designed with modality and durability in mind. They are specially manufactured for rough use in the most extreme climates, they are exposed to long resistance tests, so they withstand corrosion, saltwater and freezing, which makes them perfect as the main construction material.

Why months if it could be weeks?

Shipping containers make the construction process much easier, faster and above all more efficient than ever, they have great versatility and they can be complemented with premium interior materials without neglecting durability and elegance. Visualize the containers as a large box of legos that give you the opportunity to build anything for any purpose with a large number of possibilities, now the limit is up to you. Best of all, construction is ten times faster than conventional.

Technology and nature together

Who said technology is at odds with the natural and ecological? We made the perfect balance between both worlds possible, we achieved it by integrating recyclable materials with home automation, so your new home will not only be eco-friendly, but you can also have full control from your smartphone.
